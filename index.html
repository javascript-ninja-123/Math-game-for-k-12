Team Bio

Hey there! We know what it's like to struggle with math, as everyone has been there at some point. Not to worry though, as that's why our team has been developing a learning platform to help students all over the world with their math skills. It's who we are and what we do. We want each and every one of you to excel in math. With our intended focus on 8th Grade to High School level math, Mathematica is an interactive math website that allows students to return to the fundamental skills in math that are necessary in solving higher level problems. Our goal is to make sure that students not just memorize the concepts temporarily, but master and be able to utilize them in the future as well.
